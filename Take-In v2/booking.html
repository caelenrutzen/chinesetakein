Class Pricing

When you pay the $40, you pay for both classes. We like to keep the entrance price cheap for our students, so that they will have funds left over for grocery shopping for ingredients. This price applies to two students because we like our students to work with a partner. This helps build a bond between two chefs, and allows our students to learn how working in a team environment can help a kitchen run efficiently.

Class Booking

In order to sign up for a class, you need to check the class schedule. If the Monday/Wednesday class is filled up for the week, you can either sign up for the Tuesday/Thursday class (assuming it is also not filled up), or sign up for the Monday/Wednesday class for the following week. We recommend that you sign up for the classes a few weeks ahead of time, so that you and your partner can schedule a few hours free for each class!

Egg Rolls

A cabbage and pork dish wrapped in a egg dough and deep fried to golden brown. This class is an intermediate level class and is recommended for people with experience in cooking.

Stir Fry

A popular dish that includes Broccoli, Carrots, Onions, and more along with a protein of choice. This class is a beginner course and is recommended for people wanting to learn cooking for the first time.

Sushi

A popular dish that has many different varieties includes different proteins and vegetables. This class is a intermediate course and is recommended for people who have experience in the kitchen or have taken our classes before.

Ramen

Ramen is an advanced, but delicious dish that everyone should know how to make by hand instead of by the package. It is customizable to the person making it and has been a favorite food for a lot of people. This class is an advanced class and is only recommended for people with a lot of experience in the kitchen and feels comfortable cooking asian cuisine.
